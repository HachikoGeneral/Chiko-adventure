Game Theory

Classic gametheory dapps reinvented

1: Get some GTT

Buy some GTT shares with CHK. When you do this, you'll incur a 10% Dividend Fee, paid out to existing GTT holders. The more GTT shares you hold, the larger percentage of the dividends you are paid.


2: Refer Others to Play

Share your referral link around to friends, family and fellow degens - when they use it to buy some GTT shares, you'll earn a bonus of 3% of their total deposit, as CHK dividends! Spread the word about GTT and enjoy!


3: Compound your Earnings

Reinvesting your dividends is the fastest way to earn more in this game - doing this every day is almost akin to "compound interest". Are you a godly chad investor?


4: Sell GTT & Withdraw CHK

When you're done earning dividends, you can sell your GTT shares and withdraw the underlying collateralised CHK. Don't worry, you can join in again and start earning any time!


GTT Gauntlet

How to be a Godly Chad Stronghand!

1: Create your Gauntlet

Choose a number of days to lock your GTT away for. Don't worry, the Gauntlet does not affect your everyday GTT - you'll be depositing CHK to get GTT for the gauntlet in step 2!


2: Get GTT with CHK

Add some GTT to your Gauntlet by depositing CHK - your Gauntlet will buy GTT from the contract for you, and lock them away until your set day!


3: Compound your Earnings

Reinvesting your Gauntlet dividends is the fastest way to earn more in this game - doing this every day is almost akin to "compound interest". Show your strong hands with Gauntlet mode!


4: Sell GTT & Withdraw CHK

When the Gauntlet lock-time has elapsed, you'll be able to sell the GTT within it and withdraw CHK to your wallet! Of course, you also have the option to add more days to your Gauntlet, and start another cycle!
